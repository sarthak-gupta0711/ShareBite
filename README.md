Sharebite is a corporate food ordering platform that helps companies provide meals as an employee perk. 
It connects workplaces with local restaurants, streamlines group ordering, and simplifies meal delivery for teams.

Key points:

1) Designed for businesses, not individual consumer ordering
2) Enables companies to offer curated lunch and dinner benefits
3) Supports team orders, bulk meals, and office catering
4) Integrates with workplace perks programs and expense workflows
5) Focused on convenience, transparency, and local restaurant selection
